This will be an add-on in GoogleSheet for Finance to retrieve Delivery Trip data from sentinel system.

  • GetDTRbyDate(start_date, end_date, overwrite(boolean))To get delivery trip records by date range. if overwrite parameter is yes, then overwrite, otherwise, skip the cell(with Yellow background colour )

  • GetDTRbyBook(booking_number) To get records for a particular booking number.

Function:

  1. To get delivery trip data from sentinel system and fill in GoogleSheet by indicating start date and end date. fields are listed below.

  2. If a cell in GoogleSheet is manually reserved(with yellow background colour )
