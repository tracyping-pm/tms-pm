Background

When a trip is completed, clients/shipper are expected to review the trip or report an issue in shipper app. These reviews will be used for trucker performance analysis and customer service improvement purposes. Management will be alerted when an issue is reported or when client submit less than 3 stars.

Objective

  1. To build to client review function is shipper app.

  2. To build admin pages to share review link, view reviews, and enter resolution if follow up is conducted.

Process Logic

  1. The review window opens once a trip is marked as completed and closes 14days after.

  2. If shipper did not submit reviews, the trip will be marked as 5 star automatically. To differentiate 5 star from shipper, system should record that the 5 star is given by system.

  3. Admin can enter resolution anytime, there is no window restrictions from admin side.

  4. When shipper reported an issue, or shipper give less than 3 star review, notification will be trigger to users whose role in system is logistic managers(albert@inteluck.com, wilson@inteluck.com, kevin@inteluck.com )
